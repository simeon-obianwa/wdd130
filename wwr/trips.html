Half Day Adventure

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate

Perfect for first-timers and families! This half-day trip takes you through scenic canyons with gentle rapids and one exciting Class III rapid to get your adrenaline pumping. Includes professional guides, all safety equipment, and a riverside snack.

  • 4 hours on the water
  • Class II-III rapids
  • Perfect for families and beginners
  • All equipment provided
  • Includes light refreshments

Full Day Expedition

Duration: 8 hours | Difficulty: Intermediate

Our most popular trip! Spend a full day conquering the legendary Forked River with its mix of thrilling rapids and serene stretches. Navigate through the famous "Devil's Teeth" rapid and enjoy a gourmet riverside lunch prepared by our guides. Perfect for those seeking a complete rafting experience.

  • 8 hours of adventure
  • Class III-IV rapids
  • Gourmet riverside lunch included
  • Professional photography package
  • Transportation to/from river

Price: $169 per person

Overnight Wilderness Trip

Duration: 2 days | Difficulty: Advanced

For the true adventurer! This two-day expedition takes you deep into the wilderness where few have ventured. Camp under the stars, tackle the notorious "Thunder Falls" rapid, and experience the raw power of nature. Includes all meals, camping gear, and expert guides who know these waters like the back of their hand.

  • 2 days, 1 night on the river
  • Class IV-V rapids
  • All camping gear provided
  • Gourmet wilderness meals
  • Small group (max 8 people)

Price: $399 per person
